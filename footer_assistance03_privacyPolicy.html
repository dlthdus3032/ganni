  • Legal basis for collecting, processing and storage of your data

    GANNI cannot collect, process, store, or use, etc. your personal data without a legal basis in the EU General Data Protection Regulation (“GDPR”). The legal basis can be your consent if the processing is necessary in order to fulfill our contractual relationship, a legal obligation, or based on our legitimate interest as a company.

    We process personal data only to fulfill our contractual obligations or to preserve our overriding legitimate interests. Our legitimate interests are the implementation of our business purpose.

    Insofar as we obtain consent from the data subject for processing operations of personal data, GDPR Article 6 (1) (a) serves as the legal basis for the processing of personal data.

    Insofar as the processing of personal data is required to fulfill a legal requirement that our company is subject to, GDPR Article 6 (1) (c) serves as the legal basis.

    In the case that the vital interests of the data subject or another natural person make the processing of personal data necessary, GDPR Article 6 (1) (d) serves as the legal basis.

    If processing is required to protect a legitimate interest of our company or of a third party and the interests do not override the interests, fundamental rights, and freedoms of the data subject of the first-named interest, GDPR Article 6 (1) (f) serves as the legal basis for the processing.
